Lasagna's recipe

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Cover with foil and bake.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
