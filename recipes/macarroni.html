Macarroni's recipe

Description

Really popular as a side dish for a barbeque or just about any gathering you can think of.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package uncooked rotini pasta
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup buffalo wing sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ cup red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup red onion, diced

Steps

  1. ill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil over high heat.
  2. Stir together the mayonnaise, blue cheese dressing, buffalo wing sauce, salt and pepper in a large bowl.
